# Marketing Strategy Summary

## Target Audience

- Knowledge workers (researchers, writers, students)
- Professionals (developers, designers, marketers)
- Casual users wanting better bookmark organization

## Value Proposition

"Remember what you saved, reflect on what you learned."

Native browser extension that helps you track and review your recent bookmarks, perfect for monthly reflection and productivity workflows.

## MVP Launch Strategy (3 Months)

### Pre-Launch (Month 1)

- Build in public on Twitter/X and Reddit
- Create landing page with email signup
- Recruit 100+ beta testers
- Gather feedback and testimonials

### Launch (Month 2)

- Product Hunt launch (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Reddit posts (r/SideProject, r/productivity, r/firefox, r/chrome)
- Chrome Web Store & Firefox Add-ons publication
- Twitter/X announcement with demo
- Submit to directories (AlternativeTo, etc.)

### Post-Launch (Month 3+)

- Content marketing (blog posts, tutorials)
- Community building (Discord/email list)
- SEO optimization
- Partnership outreach
- Regular feature updates

## Marketing Channels

### Primary (Free)

- Product Hunt
- Reddit (5+ relevant subreddits)
- Twitter/X (build in public)
- Hacker News (Ask HN, Show HN)
- Browser store listings (SEO optimized)

### Secondary (Low Cost)

- Medium/Dev.to articles
- YouTube demo video
- Landing page with SEO
- Email newsletter
- Open source communities

### Long-term

- Guest posts on tech blogs
- Partnerships with productivity tools
- Referral program
- Public collections (SEO benefit)

## Key Metrics

- 5,000+ installs in 6 months
- 4.5+ star rating
- 70%+ weekly active users
- 5-10% conversion to premium (if applicable)

## Store Listing Keywords

- "recent bookmarks"
- "bookmark manager"
- "bookmark organizer"
- "productivity tool"
- "reading list"
- "bookmark history"

## Success Factors

✅ Simple, focused value proposition
✅ Solve real problem (monthly review)
✅ Cross-platform (Chrome + Firefox + Edge)
✅ Beautiful, modern interface
✅ Privacy-focused (no cloud by default)
✅ Fast and lightweight
